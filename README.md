# 🍛 Surplix – AI-Powered Food Redistribution Platform

## 🌟 Overview

Surplix is a real-time, location-based platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting surplus food to nearby people, NGOs, and even farms.

> “We don’t create more food — we make sure existing food reaches the right place, at the right time.”

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

> “Good food is thrown away every day, while people nearby are still searching for their next meal.”

At small-scale sources like **weddings, PGs, and home gatherings**, large quantities of perfectly edible food go to waste simply because there is no fast and practical way to redistribute it.

This is not just an inconvenience — it is a **real-world redistribution problem**.  
Food exists. People need it. But there is no system connecting them in time.

---

## 💡 Solution

Surplix provides a smart redistribution system where surplus food is efficiently connected to nearby users.

- 🍛 Users can list extra food  
- 📍 Location is auto-detected  
- 👥 Nearby buyers can claim food at low cost  
- 🏢 NGOs are prioritized if food remains unclaimed  
- 🚜 Farms collect food if it is no longer consumable  

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📍 Smart Location Mapping
- Auto-detects location while listing food  
- Buyers can set preferred pickup radius  
- Default limit: ~5 km for buyers  

---

### 💰 Reverse Auction Pricing
- Price drops at fixed time intervals (e.g., every 10 minutes)  
- Encourages faster claiming  
- Ensures food doesn’t go to waste  

---

### 🧩 Fractional Claiming
- Buyers can claim only the quantity they need  
- Prevents over-purchasing and secondary waste  

---

### 🏢 NGO Priority System
- If food remains unclaimed → NGOs are notified  

---

### 📦 Pickup Coordination System
- No delivery → direct pickup model  

Provides:
- 📍 Exact location (Google Maps)  
- 📞 Seller contact  
- 📝 Instructions  

---

### 📊 Seller Dashboard
- Track listed food  

Monitor:
- Quantity claimed  
- Remaining food  
- Pickup status  

---

### 🏢 NGO Dashboard
- View available food listings  
- Accept & reserve food  
- Access full pickup details  
- Confirm collection  

---

### 🌐 Multi-language Support
- Supports multiple languages  
- Improves accessibility and usability  

---

### 🤖 AI Integration
- AI-based food detection during listing  


---

## ♻️ Zero Waste Extension (Farm Redistribution)

Surplix ensures that no food goes to waste at any stage.

If food is no longer suitable for human consumption:

- 🌾 Nearby farms are notified  
- 🚜 Farms collect the food  
- 🐄 Food is repurposed as animal feed  

> “Even if food cannot feed people, we make sure it still feeds life.”

---

## 🎯 How It Works

1. User lists surplus food  
2. Location is auto-detected  
3. Nearby users can view and claim food  
4. Price reduces over time  
5. If unclaimed → NGOs are notified  
6. If still unused → farms collect it  
7. Food is successfully redistributed with zero waste  

---

## 💥 What Makes Us Different

Most platforms treat food waste as a **convenience problem**  
→ offering discounts and deals  

**Surplix treats it as a redistribution problem**

- ✔ Focus on real-time, local matching  
- ✔ Direct pickup model (no delivery dependency)  
- ✔ NGO fallback system ensures food reaches people  
- ✔ Farm-level redistribution ensures zero waste  
- ✔ End-to-end system from listing → claiming → pickup  
- ✔ AI used for decision-making, not just features
